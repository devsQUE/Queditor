Privacy Policy – Queditor App
Last Updated: 29 August 2025

devsQUE (“we”, “our”, “us”) built the Queditor app as a code editor for developers. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect
- Queditor does not collect personal information such as name, email, or contact details.
- All your projects, files, and data are stored locally on your device (using IndexedDB or local storage).
- We may collect non-identifiable technical data (such as app version, crash logs, or performance data) if you choose to share feedback.

2. How We Use Information
- Since we do not collect personal data, there is no selling, renting, or sharing of your data.
- Any permissions requested (like file access) are solely used for app functionality (import/export projects).

3. Data Storage
- Your data remains on your device. We do not upload files or projects to our servers.
- If you uninstall the app, your locally stored projects may be deleted permanently.

4. Security
- Since your projects are stored locally, you are responsible for backups.
- We take reasonable measures in the app to safeguard against unauthorized access, but no system is 100% secure.

5. Children’s Privacy
- Queditor does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
- If you believe a child has provided data, please contact us to remove it.

6. Third-Party Services
- Queditor may use third-party open-source libraries (e.g., CodeMirror, Prettier, JSZip). These libraries may have their own privacy practices.
- We do not share your data with third parties.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

- Continued use of Queditor after updates means you accept the new policy.
